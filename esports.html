Esports Broadcast Production

Telling the Story

  • An esports game observer is responsible for directing the in-game camera to ensure the audience gets the best view of the action. Observers focus on significant in-game events, including crucial kills, strategic maneuvers, and team fights, to ensure they are showcased. They help narrate the unfolding game by choosing perspectives that highlight the ongoing narrative, such as following a player on a streak or showcasing a team's strategy. Observers work closely with all production teams to ensure smooth and coherent broadcast.
